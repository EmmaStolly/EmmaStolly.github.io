about me

I'm currently a 20 year old student originally from Somerset studying Digital Media Design at the University of Plymouth. I have a BTEC in Digital Media Design as well from Bridgwater and Taunton College.

A big passion of mine is creating artwork. I love drawing in Photoshop with my Surface Pro, but also enjoy creating traditional art of all forms, my favourite medium being watercolour. In my Digital Media Design course I'm also learning new technologies through my various modules, where I am developing my skills in different areas. Through my University course, I have learnt a lot about web development and am now confident in my HTML & CSS skills, as well as being intermediate at javascript. I have also developed hardware skills through the use of Arduinos, and have managed to build a mood tracker and a lie detector in the past. Through my 'Digital Media Design' module and through personal practice I have also largely developed my Adobe Creative Cloud skills. My other hobbies include reading and gaming - specifically League of Legends (Which is why I enjoy creating fan art of the game!)
